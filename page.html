Idle Mining Empire puts you at the helm of a mining business. Start from scratch and work up to running a fully automated mining empire that generates huge profits!

How to Play Idle Mining Empire

Mine resources

Click on your worker to produce resources for selling. You can then use your elevator to transport the rocks to the ground, where they will be transported to the warehouse. As you get further in the game, you can dig deeper and unlock mine shafts, which generates more money.

Upgrade and automate

It wouldn't be an Idle Mining Empire without automation. Early in the game, you can start adding managers to each part of the workflow. You can also upgrade each part to move faster and produce higher profits!

Features

  • Build a mining empire from scratch
  • Unlock new mining shafts
  • Upgrade your workers
  • Automate the workflow with managers

FAQ

What do investors do in Idle Mining Empire?

When you sell the business to investors, the game starts again with a multiplier added.

How can I progress quickly in Idle Mining Empire?

Focus on upgrading and building your automated earnings.

How do you collect cash in Idle Mining Empire?

You earn cash with most work tasks in Idle Mining Empire. You don't have to actively collect the cash; simply complete the task, and the cash will be added to your total amount earned.
